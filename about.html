History Fit-2-Go!

Fit-2-Go was founded in 2015 by Vuyo Xashimba. Mr. Xashimba used to work as a personal trainer on behalf of a local Cape Town gym.
After seeing how much misinformation about fitness had been spread online, he wanted to create a way for people to have accurate, scientifically-backed info right at their fingertips.
It was then that he founded Fit-2-Go as a subsidiary of that very same gym.

Initially, Fit-2-Go operated locally, handing out pamphlets and holidng talks in schools or community centres educating people on the importance of proper dieting and regular exercise.

However, Mr Xashimba felt that Fit-2-Go was not living up to its potential.
He bought the rights to Fit-2-Go from the gym in 2019 and enlisted the help of experts in many fields to turn Fit-2-Go into the company that it is today.

Fit-2-Go's Vision

Ever since its inception, Fit-2-Go has been about honesty and accessibility. Our vision here at Fit-2-Go is for the company to be one of the most accurate, reliable and accessible sources of fitness and diet info in South Africa.
